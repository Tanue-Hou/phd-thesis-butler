# Top 50 Templates — SURVEY

### 1. Therefore, a gap exists in the development of ___ (e.g., comprehensive methodologies) for ___ (e.g., the blade processing of internal cycloidal surfaces) considering ___ (e.g., both geometric accuracy and surface quality).
- subtype: gap
- when_to_use: 在第一章分析末尾，自然过渡到研究目标。
- score: 2

### 2. An analysis of the technological capabilities of modern methods for forming ___ reveals that ___ (e.g., method A) provides ___ while limited in ___.
- subtype: analysis_of_methods
- when_to_use: 用于文献综述章节，系统评述相关工作。
- score: 2

### 3. Prior work on ___ (e.g., machining of complex surfaces) is often limited by ___ (e.g., the geometric capabilities of tools or low productivity).
- subtype: limitations_of_prior
- when_to_use: 在分析章节中，用于论证研究缺口。
- score: 2

### 4. Despite extensive research on analog phased arrays, the specific challenges of ___ for airborne digital arrays remain insufficiently addressed.
- subtype: gap
- when_to_use: Gap identification paragraph
- score: 2

### 5. While ___ [method A] offers ___ [advantage], it suffers from ___ [disadvantage]. In contrast, ___ [method B] addresses this by ___ [feature].

- when_to_use: 文献综述章节，对现有工作进行系统性比较。
- score: 2

### 6. Сравнительный анализ существующих методов ___ показал, что подходы на основе ___ обладают преимуществами в части ___, однако уступают по ___.

- when_to_use: Comparing survey entries systematically

### 7. Технико-технологические характеристики [интеллектуальной экономики] как экономической деятельности на основе ___ исследовали ___, ___ и др.
- subtype: evaluation_axes
- when_to_use: 在文献综述中，介绍关于研究对象（智能经济）在具体技术实现层面的研究。

### 8. Existing computational methods for [PROBLEM TYPE] include [METHOD_A] and [METHOD_B], which differ in their [CRITERION_1] and [CRITERION_2].

- score: 2

### 9. However, a comprehensive approach that integrates ___ for different types of mesh shells (cylindrical, conical) remains underdeveloped.
