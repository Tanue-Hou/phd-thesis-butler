# Top 50 Templates — CONCLUSION

### 1. In summary, this work successfully modeled the formation and evolution of ___, demonstrating their substantial impact on ___ in critical flight phases.
- subtype: summary
- when_to_use: Opening the conclusion chapter to recap the essence of the work.
- score: 2
